About Us

When you come to ABC Church, expect to find a community that is kind, patient, and positively passionate about God. Here we strive to make a difference in the world and support all of those who come through our doors. Through worship, prayer, and acts of service, we hope to learn and embody the kindness and care of God. Our community is made possible through our members' worship and support, bringing our commitment to Jesus Christ to light.

Come and see for yourself, and learn to embrace community and service through your worship of Christ.
